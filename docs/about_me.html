About Me

My name is Jaylen Price (JP). I'm a former D1 athlete from Dallas, Texas who moved to Ventura to be with my family...

As a duel threat athlete in high school, I held high ranks on the football field and was a state competitor in track. My senior year I made the tough decision to turn down multiple track scholarships to continue my football career at Montana State University. There I studied Kinesiology where I developed a passion for fitness and coaching. I went on to obtain a job as a physical therapy technician where I learned the important balance between performance and recovery.

Through my experience as an athlete I found that speed and agility training can advance an athlete's potential to perform at a higher level of competition. My mission is to provide our members with tailored training methods that will minimize the risk of injury while maximizing strength, speed, and endurance. In my free time I enjoy hiking, playing beach volleyball and spending time with my family. I pride in building relationships that motivate and encourage athletes to challenge themselves day in and day out.
